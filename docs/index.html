Bank vs Finance Co. State banks (BOC, People's, NSB) carry a government guarantee; private banks usually pay more. LOLC is a licensed finance company (LFC), not a bank — higher rated risk, but deposits are CBSL-insured up to Rs 1.1m per customer.
How it updates. A scheduled job fetches each bank's official rate page daily and rewrites the data this page reads. Banks marked stale have bot-protected or JavaScript-rendered sites — their rows fall back to last-known values and are refreshed via a browser-based fetch.
Payout & tax. Rates shown are interest-at-maturity. Monthly payout options are usually slightly lower. FD interest is subject to Withholding Tax (WHT) deducted at source — compare net returns.
Longer tenures pay more. Several banks offer higher rates beyond 2 years and on special day-products (e.g. 100/200/300/400-day FDs) and senior-citizen / bulk deposits — ask the branch.
Disclaimer: Rates are indicative and change frequently without notice. This is not financial advice — always confirm the prevailing rate, minimum, payout terms and WHT directly with the bank before depositing.
